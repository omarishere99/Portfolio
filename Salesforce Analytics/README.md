# Salesforce Service Cloud Analytics — IDnow GmbH

**Tools:** Salesforce CRM · Service Cloud · Custom Report Builder · Formula Fields · SLA Logic

---

## Context

Built a suite of operational analytics dashboards for a BaFin-regulated identity verification SaaS platform, supporting a service desk team managing enterprise clients in the German banking and financial services sector. Dashboards were used by team leads and senior management for daily operational oversight and weekly business reviews.

---

## Dashboards Built

### 1. Service Level Dashboard (V4)

Tracks SLA compliance across the full case lifecycle — first reply and resolution compliance rates, SLA achievement by service level tier (Business vs Enterprise) and priority, and SLA compliance history over time. Designed to give operations leadership an immediate read on whether contractual service commitments are being met.

![Service Level Dashboard](screenshots/service_level_dashboard_v4.png)

---

### 2. Case Overview — Management View (V2)

Executive-level pipeline dashboard covering case creation vs resolution trends, open cases by type/priority/status, case resolution time trends, queue workload distribution, and top accounts by open case volume. Used by senior management for weekly business rhythm reviews.

![Case Overview Management Dashboard](screenshots/case_overview_management_v2.png)

---

### 3. Service Desk Dashboard — Team Lead View (V3)

Operational team lead dashboard tracking open cases by owner and status, aged cases untouched for 5+ days, hours remaining to first reply per case, IT-linked Jira tickets, data quality issues (missing contact/type/product information), and individual cases processed history. Designed to give team leads real-time visibility into workload and risk.

![Service Desk Team Lead Dashboard](screenshots/service_desk_team_lead_v3.png)

---

## What These Dashboards Enabled

- Identified SLA breach risks before they occurred through hours-to-first-reply tracking
- Surfaced data quality issues that were causing misrouting and reporting inaccuracies
- Gave management a single source of truth for case pipeline health across enterprise clients
- Supported weekly business reviews with structured, actionable operational metrics
